April 9th, 2020 - Strive to Try

To everyone on the Internet, hello! I am in the works of becoming a web-developer of some sort. To start, I began my journey in Skillcrush's "Break Into Tech" program. I enjoyed all the lessons and videos on how they broke each concept down. Coding is definitely like learning a new language and a sport. With each lesson, you have to try and attempt to use what you've learned in multiple areas.

Everyone can be a great student, but what divides are those who can transform those concepts into wonderful products. The big mountain I am getting over each day I code, is fear and failure. I strive for perfection with the hopes I get things right the first time. Coding doesn't truly follow that concept. Trial and error is my new best friend and my new normal. I am re-wiring my brain to simply enjoy the process.
Each day comes with less fear as I tackle on little portions of web development that will make the bigger picture beautiful.

Until then, I will document my journey here. I will enjoy the process and not look too far ahead.
